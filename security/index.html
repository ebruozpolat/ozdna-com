OZDNA.COM — SECURITY

Safe by
construction.

The strongest security control is the risk you never take on. ozDNA is designed so the most dangerous things simply aren't in the system: no custody of funds, no wallets holding value, and — in the free flow — no image leaving your device to be signed.

[01]WHAT WE DON'T TOUCH
  • No user funds, everozDNA never custodies, brokers, or moves user money, and holds no wallet of value. Timestamp anchoring is batched and paid by us — nothing in the product moves anyone's money.
  • On-device signingOriginDNA's free flow signs images in your browser via WebAssembly. The image is never uploaded to us to be signed, so there's nothing to leak on that path.
  • Provenance, not detectionThere are no AI-detection classifiers ingesting your content in v1. We record origin; we don't run a black-box verdict on your media.
  • Data residency for ComplyDNARegulated institutions can run ComplyDNA fully on-premises, keeping sensitive compliance data inside their own boundary.
[02]PLATFORM HARDENING
TRANSPORT

HTTPS + HSTS

The site is served over HTTPS only, with HSTS preload, and http/www variants are redirected to the canonical host.

HEADERS

Locked down

Responses set X-Content-Type-Options, X-Frame-Options: DENY, a strict Referrer-Policy, and a restrictive Permissions-Policy.

SURFACE

Minimal

Internal strategy, source and platform paths are 404'd at the edge and never served on the public domain.

[03]RESPONSIBLE DISCLOSURE

Found something?
Tell us first.

If you believe you've found a vulnerability, email security@ozdna.com. Please give us a reasonable window to investigate and fix before any public disclosure, and don't access or modify data that isn't yours while testing.

Our machine-readable policy lives at /.well-known/security.txt (RFC 9116). We're a small team and read every report — good-faith researchers are credited here on request.

Pre-build note. OriginDNA is not yet in production. This page describes how the product and site are being built to be safe; it will grow into full operational detail as services go live.
