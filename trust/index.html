OZDNA.COM — TRUST CENTER

Verify,
don't trust.

Our whole approach to trust is to remove the need for it. ozDNA is built by Findbelow Ventures across two products — ComplyDNA and OriginDNA — and both are designed so every output ships with the evidence to check it yourself, rather than a claim you have to take on faith.

[01]VERIFICATION OVER "TRUST US"

Every output
carries its evidence.

The point of both products is verifiability. ComplyDNA answers Turkish financial-compliance questions with inline statutory citations, so a reviewer can follow each claim back to the source text instead of trusting a black box. OriginDNA gives an image a checkable record of origin — a signature at creation and an anchored timestamp — so a third party can confirm where a file came from without taking anyone's word for it.

We would rather hand you a link to check than ask you to believe us. That is the whole design.

[02]WHAT WE DON'T DO
  • Provenance, not detectionOriginDNA v1 ships no AI-detection classifiers. We record and verify where content came from; we do not run a guess-the-fake verdict over your media. Provenance is checkable; detection is a black box, and we won't pretend otherwise.
  • No custody of fundsozDNA never holds, brokers, or moves user money, and keeps no wallet of value. There is no token, and none is planned. The only thing anchored to a chain is a batched hash we pay for — invisible plumbing, not a crypto product.
  • No overclaimed certificationsWe are pre-build and hold no third-party audits. We do not claim SOC 2, ISO 27001, or GDPR/KVKK "certification." ComplyDNA is built for KVKK and MASAK contexts; that is a design goal, not a certificate.
  • Images only, for nowOriginDNA v1 covers still images (JPG/PNG) only. Video, audio, and mobile capture are explicitly later, not quietly implied.

[04]THE C2PA CAVEAT

What we can
honestly claim.

OriginDNA uses C2PA Content Credentials, but we are careful about what that means today. C2PA is not mandated by name in the EU rules. Until ozDNA passes the C2PA Conformance Program, our Content Credentials read as "unknown source" in the official C2PA Verify tool — so we do not promise "trusted Content Credentials."

Instead, v1 ships its own verify page plus an independent chain anchor for the timestamp. That is what you can actually check today, and it is all we claim. We would rather under-promise on a standard's trust list than imply an endorsement we haven't earned.

Pre-build note. OriginDNA is not yet in production — nothing described here is live. The product is in blueprint stage ahead of an October 2026 MVP. This page states how it is being built to be verifiable, and will grow into operational detail as services go live.
