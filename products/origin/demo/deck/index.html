03 — The solution

One engine. Three steps.

[01]

Classify

Deterministic risk-tier logic over EU AI Act Art. 5 (prohibited), Annex III (high-risk) and Art. 50 (transparency). Transparent rules — not a black-box guess.

[02]

Oblige

Each tier generates its exact obligations with article citations, plus a human-oversight log entry (Art. 14) recording who signed off and when.

[03]

Anchor

The assessment is hashed (real SHA-256) and written to an immutable audit record anchored to Solana. Tamper with it and the hash no longer matches.

Same core as OriginDNA's image provenance — sign, timestamp, fingerprint — repurposed from a pixel to a decision. A working demo is live at ozdna.com/products/origin/demo/.
