The compliance engine
for auditable AI.

Describe any AI system — an autonomous agent, a tokenized-RWA workflow, a recruitment screener, an image generator. The engine classifies its risk tier under the EU AI Act, generates the exact obligations that attach to that tier, logs the human-oversight decision, and anchors an immutable audit record. Same provenance engine that gives an image a DNA — pointed at a decision instead of a pixel.

[01]

Classify

Deterministic risk-tier logic over EU AI Act Art. 5 (prohibited), Annex III (high-risk) and Art. 50 (transparency). Transparent rules, not a black-box guess.

[02]

Oblige

Each tier generates its own obligations checklist with article citations, plus a human-oversight log entry (Art. 14) recording who signed off and when.

[03]

Anchor

The assessment is hashed (real SHA-256) and written to an immutable audit record, anchored to Solana. Tamper with the record and the hash no longer matches.

What's real, what's simulated

Honesty is the product. Here is exactly what this demo computes for real and what it stands in for.

✓ Real in this demo

  • The risk-tier classification logic — deterministic rules mapped to EU AI Act Art. 5, Annex III and Art. 50.
  • The obligations checklist generated per tier, with article citations.
  • The SHA-256 fingerprint of the assessment — computed in your browser with the Web Crypto API. Change one field and the hash changes.
  • The canonical, downloadable assessment record (JSON) that the hash is taken over.

◑ Simulated (production layer)

  • The Solana anchor — transaction signature, slot and block time are derived deterministically from the real hash for the demo. Nothing is broadcast to any network.
  • In production the same hash is submitted in a batched memo/PDA write on Solana; the audit record then carries a verifiable, look-up-able transaction — no token, no user funds, we pay the anchor fee.
  • Legal classification here is illustrative engineering logic, not legal advice. A production engine keeps the Annex III / Art. 50 mapping versioned against the current consolidated text.

OriginDNA is pre-launch. This page demonstrates the compliance-engine concept and is not a certified compliance tool.
