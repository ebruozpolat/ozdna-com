[02]DETECTION

Multi-dimensional
AI risk analysis

Most detectors return a single percentage. Ozdna evaluates text across four distinct dimensions. You can surface all metrics in your API response or use a targeted subset — only what your product actually needs.

MetricWhat it measures
Overall Risk Score Probability of AI generation, scored 0–1
Repetition & Rhythm LLM-characteristic sentence length patterns and flow repetition
Filler & Homogeneity Redundant filler phrases and overly uniform sentence structure
Contextual Coherence Intra-paragraph consistency and human-like variation
