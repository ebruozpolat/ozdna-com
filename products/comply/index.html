Every claim traceable to a madde.

Unlike general-purpose LLMs, ComplyDNA does not paraphrase from memory. Each sentence carries an inline [TEBLİĞ / Madde] reference. Responses end with a full source list and effective-date metadata — so your team can verify every claim at sentence level before it reaches a regulator or board.
