[04]FAQ
What products does ozDNA offer?

Two products under one principle: every output carries the evidence to check it. ComplyDNA answers Turkish compliance questions with article-level statutory citations. OriginDNA gives images a provable origin for EU AI Act Article 50 content marking.

What is the regulatory deadline?

August 2, 2026 — EU AI Act Article 50 and California SB 942 apply. December 2, 2026 — marking grace expires for existing GenAI systems. OriginDNA's MVP ships in October 2026 and launches into that December wave.

Who is this for?

ComplyDNA: Turkish compliance teams. OriginDNA: GenAI apps that must mark content (primary), marketplace sellers and creators (secondary), newsrooms and fact-checkers (free forever), and later legal/enterprise evidence capture.

Is OriginDNA an AI detector?

No — deliberately. Detectors guess after the fact. OriginDNA works from the other end: content proves where it came from at creation. Provenance, not detection.

Is there a token or crypto wallet?

No wallet, no token, nothing to buy or hold — and no token is planned. Both products are normal SaaS. We never touch user funds.
