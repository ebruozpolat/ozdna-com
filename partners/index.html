[02]OPEN STANDARDS — PROVENANCE

Built on open
standards.

OriginDNA builds on the open content-provenance ecosystem: the C2PA specification and the Content Authenticity Initiative (CAI) community. These are open standards for signing where a piece of content came from — the same foundations a growing number of tools are adopting.

To be precise about where we stand: we are building on C2PA and planning to join the CAI. We are not a certified member, an official partner, or endorsed by them — C2PA conformance is a program we have not yet passed, and joining CAI is on our list, not done. We will only claim status once it's real.

SPEC / C2PA

C2PA

An open standard for cryptographically signing content provenance. OriginDNA signs images against it and anchors a timestamp so the record can't be backdated.

COMMUNITY / CAI

CAI

The Content Authenticity Initiative — an open community around provenance tooling. Joining is planned; we build in the open alongside it.

[03]WHO WE WORK WITH

Fact-checkers free.
Partners welcome.

Fact-checking organizations get OriginDNA free, forever. They are flagship users, not a revenue line — provenance is most useful where it's used to verify, and we'd rather it be in those hands than behind a paywall.

We're also open to working with integrators, resellers, and compliance-tooling partners — teams building products where verifiable image origin is a natural fit. If that's you, reach out and tell us what you're building.

  • Fact-checkers & newsrooms Free, forever — flagship users of the verify flow.
  • Integrators Build OriginDNA signing and verification into your product.
  • Resellers Bring provenance to your own customer base.
  • Compliance tooling Pair image marking with your EU AI Act / disclosure workflows.
OriginDNA is pre-build — nothing is live yet and partnerships are still forming. If any of the above fits, reach out to explore; we'd rather talk early.
