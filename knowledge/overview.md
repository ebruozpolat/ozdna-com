# ozDNA — Verifiable by design

ozDNA (Findbelow Ventures) builds software for one problem: **trusting what AI produces**.
Every product follows the same principle — an output must carry the evidence needed to
verify it. No claim without a source; no content without an origin.

## The two products

**ComplyDNA** (RegTech, Türkçe) — a compliance LLM for Turkish financial regulation
(MASAK communiqués, AML/CFT laws 5549 & 6415, KVKK). Every sentence in every answer
carries an inline statutory citation in the form `[TEBLİĞ / Madde]`, so compliance teams
can verify the exact article behind each claim. Cloud API or fully on-premises.
Status: early access / pilot program.

**OriginDNA** (content provenance, global) — infrastructure that gives every image a
"DNA": a cryptographic signature at creation (open C2PA standard), a timestamp anchored
in a public, independently verifiable record, and a perceptual fingerprint that survives
metadata stripping. Built for EU AI Act Article 50 content marking (applies Aug 2, 2026;
grace for existing GenAI systems ends Dec 2, 2026). Status: pre-launch, waitlist open.

## What ozDNA is not

- Not an AI-text detector or humanizer — no detection-evasion products exist under this brand.
- OriginDNA is provenance, not AI detection; it has no token and holds no user funds.
