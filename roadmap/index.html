OZDNA.COM — ROADMAP

The dates are
already set.

Our roadmap isn't a wish list — it's pinned to the regulatory calendar. The EU AI Act and California SB 942 fixed hard dates; ozDNA is built to meet the wave they create. Dates below are from the EU AI Act's published timeline; your obligations depend on your product and jurisdiction.

[01]THE COMPLIANCE CLOCK
Jul 22, 2026

Code of Practice list closes

The EU GenAI Code of Practice signatory list closes. Who signs is a signal — and a lead list for who needs content marking next.

Aug 2, 2026

The marking rules apply

EU AI Act Article 50 starts applying and California SB 942 takes effect the same day. Generative-AI providers must mark AI-generated content in machine-readable form. This is the PR moment.

Dec 2, 2026

The grace period ends

Marking obligations reach GenAI systems already on the market. The enforcement wave OriginDNA launches into — existing apps need marking, not promises.

Feb 2, 2027

Interoperable detection

Interoperable detection of marked content is required, with a consortium route open to smaller providers — the direction our provenance approach is built for.

OriginDNA — pre-build
